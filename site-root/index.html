Revolutionizing solar management

ecoEngin is at the forefront of solar technology, developing an innovative Solar Digital Twin platform that transforms the way solar energy systems are monitored and optimized. By crafting virtual replicas of physical installations, we provide groundbreaking solutions for performance optimization, predictive maintenance, and carbon credit verification. Our advanced technology combines physics-based modeling and machine learning to enhance energy production by 15-20% on average.

Located in Newmarket, GB, we aim to set the standard for solar system management across all installation types.
